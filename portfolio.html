About the Designer

Smt. Lakshmi Prassanna Gollapudi

Prassannaa Fashion Institute was founded by Smt. Lakshmi Prassanna Gollapudi, a passionate fashion designer who completed her B.F. Tech in Fashion Design from NIFT Hyderabad. With over 15 years of rich experience, she has trained thousands of women across the twin cities.
